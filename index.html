Intro to screenprinting

Before starting this project physically, we first had to make our screenprinting idea on photoshop, through out this process we learned how to use our creative thoughts to illustrate a short scene, while also displaying a word/phrase with a deeper meaning.For my word i chose Sonder. I choose this word because I feel as if it helps people to see in other peoples perspective. The process took time but it helped us get ready for our next step, actually making the screen print. Before doing that we were shown examples and the steps we would needed to complete to sucessfully make our screenprint artwork. In order to make our image, We need to Measure,cut,build, and finally paint all while also record to gain footage for our B-roll, knowing the task at hand I was ready to commit to finish the project.

The process

Our first step was measure and cut. We first had to find pieces of wood that would be strong and hold up for this project. After finding a sutiable piece of wood, we then had to measure and cut.First measuring, when measuring we had to first adobe illustrator to find the exact mesurment we wanted for our image, after this we used techniques to sketch these mesurments on the wood. After measuring the wood we then proceeded to cut the wood using these machines. The machines were dangerous so we had to be careful while also avoding to get wood scraps in our eye by using goggles. After finishing cutting our wood we procceded onto the text step.

Building the Frame

Our next step was building our frame for the painting. This step consist of Gluing,stappiling, and then pasting our image on mesh and using hammers to keep the frame in tact. While gluing we had to use the parts we just cut and glue thme together correctly. After we used the stapples to keep the glue from sliding around and making the frame loose(they come off later), after we had to use the hammer to keep the stapples in the wood.
